Ethical Analysis

Key ethical lenses include utilitarianism (maximizing safety and minimizing harm) and deontological ethics (ensuring fairness and adherence to laws). These perspectives guide the assessment of complex decisions such as accident avoidance algorithms.

Social Implications

Challenges include the moral programming of vehicles, job displacement in driving professions, and ensuring equitable access to the technology across demographics.
