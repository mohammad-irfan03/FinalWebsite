Recommendations for Stakeholders

Governments and Regulations

Governments must develop clear policies to ensure public safety, address liability, and regulate self-driving vehicles effectively. These regulations must keep pace with the rapidly evolving technology to ensure societal trust and acceptance.

Engineering and Innovation

Engineers need to prioritize the development of adaptive systems capable of addressing unforeseen scenarios. Incorporating feedback through public trials ensures reliability and enhances public confidence in the technology.

Collaboration and Transparency

Collaboration among policymakers, engineers, and the public is critical for the success of self-driving vehicles. Companies must foster transparency by engaging with communities and updating safety protocols based on citizen input.
