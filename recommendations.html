Recommendations to Stakeholders

  • Governments: Develop clear regulations and policies to ensure the safety and accountability of self-driving vehicles. Laws must evolve alongside technological advancements to address new challenges and risks effectively.
  • Engineers: Prioritize building adaptive and reliable systems capable of managing unforeseen scenarios. Continuous improvement and integration of public feedback through trials are essential for safety and trust.
  • Collaboration and Public Trust: Foster transparency and cooperation among stakeholders, including policymakers, engineers, and the public. Companies must engage with communities to address concerns, update safety protocols, and promote acceptance of self-driving cars.
