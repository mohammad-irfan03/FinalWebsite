Introduction to Self-Driving Cars

What is your target technology?

Self-driving cars, also known as autonomous vehicles, use advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, sensors, and machine learning to navigate roads without human intervention. They represent a significant step forward in transportation innovation.

!

Why Are They Important?

Autonomous vehicles aim to improve road safety, reduce traffic congestion, and provide mobility for those unable to drive. With the potential to save lives and transform industries, self-driving cars are a crucial innovation.

Self-driving cars application domain

The integration of self-driving cars into society raises questions about their impact on the job market, urban planning, and environmental sustainability. Exploring their potential and challenges is essential for informed decision-making.
